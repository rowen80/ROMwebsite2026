About Ryan Owen

30 Years Shooting the Eastern Shore

Ocean City based photographer Ryan Owen has been shooting the Eastern Shore for the past 30 years. Ryan's unique style provides vibrant colors and composite images that are both ethereal and realistic.

Educated at the University of Maryland and the Brooks Institute of Photography in Santa Barbara, CA, Ryan brings studio-quality lighting and technique to every listing — from condos to oceanfront estates.

FAA-certified drone operator. Nikon Z9 and Z7 camera systems. Godox professional strobes.
