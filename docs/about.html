Ryan Owen — Real Estate Photographer

Ocean City based photographer Ryan Owen has been shooting the Eastern Shore for the past 30 years. Ryan's unique style of photography provides vibrant colors and composite images that are both ethereal and realistic — a combination that makes listings stand out in a crowded market.

Ryan's work spans residential and commercial real estate across Maryland and Delaware, with a specialization in coastal and waterfront properties. He brings studio-quality lighting to every shoot, using daylight-balanced Godox strobes to eliminate the color casts and blown-out windows that plague standard real estate photography.

Educated at the University of Maryland and the Brooks Institute of Photography in Santa Barbara, CA — one of the most respected photography programs in the country — Ryan combines academic training with three decades of field experience.

As an FAA-certified drone operator, Ryan provides aerial photography and video that gives buyers a true sense of a property's location, lot, and surroundings. Drone coverage is recommended for virtually every listing.

  • Education University of Maryland; Brooks Institute of Photography, Santa Barbara, CA
  • Experience 30+ years, Eastern Shore Maryland & Delaware
  • Certifications FAA Part 107 Certified Drone Operator
  • Equipment Nikon Z9, Nikon Z7, Sony A7III, Godox professional strobes
  • Specialization Residential & commercial real estate, coastal & waterfront properties
